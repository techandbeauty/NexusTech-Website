Process & Timeline

Timeline depends on project complexity. A simple business website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex sites with custom features take 4-8 weeks. E-commerce and web applications can take 6-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline in your proposal.

Our process has three main phases: (1) Discovery - we learn about your business, goals, and requirements through a free consultation; (2) Proposal - you receive a detailed project plan with timeline and transparent pricing; (3) Build & Launch - we develop your solution with regular check-ins, then launch and provide 30 days of support.

You'll receive weekly progress updates and have access to a staging site where you can see work in progress. We're also available for questions throughout the project. Communication is key to successful projects, and we make it a priority.

To get started, we typically need: your business information and branding assets (logo, colors, etc.), content for your site (text, images), examples of sites you like, and a clear idea of your goals. Don't worry if you don't have everything ready - we can help guide you through the process.

Support & Maintenance

Every project includes 30 days of free support after launch. This covers bug fixes, minor adjustments, and questions about using your new site or app. After the initial period, we offer ongoing maintenance plans starting at $150/month.
