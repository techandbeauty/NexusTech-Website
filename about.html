About Nexus Tech

We're engineers who build technology that actually works for real businesses.

Our Story

From Help Desk to Digital Solutions Partner

Nexus Tech Digital Solutions was founded by Meisha Vernell, a software engineer with over a decade of experience in technology—starting at Apple Help Desk in 2015 and evolving into full-stack development, mobile apps, and AI integration.

After years of seeing businesses struggle with overpriced agencies, broken promises, and cookie-cutter solutions, Meisha founded Nexus Tech with a simple mission: build technology that actually works, at prices that make sense, with communication you can count on.

Today, we work with small businesses, startups, and government contractors who need custom software solutions without the enterprise price tag or agency runaround.
