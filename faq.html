Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and process.

Pricing & Payment

Website projects start at $2,500 for a basic 5-page site. Most small business websites fall in the $2,500-$7,500 range depending on complexity. We provide fixed-price quotes after our discovery call—no hourly billing, no surprise fees.

All project quotes include design, development, testing, deployment, and post-launch support (30-60 days depending on project type). You also get full ownership of all code and assets—no licensing fees or ongoing costs beyond hosting.

We typically require 50% upfront to begin work and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. We accept credit cards, ACH transfers, and checks.

Yes, for projects over $5,000 we can discuss payment plans. Contact us to discuss options that work for your budget.

Process & Timeline

Timeline varies by project type: Websites typically take 3-6 weeks. Mobile apps take 8-16 weeks. Business automation projects take 2-4 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline in your proposal.

We follow a structured process: Discovery call → Proposal (48 hours) → Design mockups → Client approval → Development → Testing → Launch → Post-launch support. You'll have visibility and input at every stage.

We handle the heavy lifting, but we do need your input at key milestones—usually a few check-ins for design approval and content review. Expect to spend 2-4 hours total over the course of a typical website project.

Every project includes 30-60 days of post-launch support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. After that, we offer ongoing support packages or can quote additional work as needed.

Technical Questions

We choose the right tool for each project. For websites: HTML/CSS/JavaScript, React, or Next.js. For mobile: Swift/SwiftUI (iOS), Kotlin (Android). For backends: Node.js, Python, Firebase. We're technology-agnostic and focus on what's best for your specific needs.

Absolutely. Upon final payment, you own 100% of all code, designs, and assets we create. No licensing fees, no restrictions. You can take it to another developer, modify it yourself, or do whatever you want with it.

Yes, we can help set up hosting and configure your domain. Hosting costs are separate (typically $10-50/month depending on needs) and are paid directly to the hosting provider. You maintain full control of your accounts.

Working Together

We believe in responsive, clear communication. You'll get same-day email responses during business hours (M-F, 9am-6pm EST). We use email primarily, but can accommodate Slack or scheduled calls as needed.

Yes! We work with clients across the United States. All communication and collaboration happens remotely via video calls, email, and screen sharing.
